ToryTechs.UK

A community of politically conservative makers and problem solvers

ToryTechs convenes right-leaning technologists, designers, politicians, and activists. We believe that a friendly and open community of makers can help improve British politics and civil service through technology and collaboration.

Join the Community View Events

Who is welcome?

Everyone is welcome in the community (yes, even lefties) as long as they are constructive and polite.
That said, we specifically cater for...

Programmers

Develop software that helps make politics more effective

Designers

Design tools, processes, apps, and websites that make Britain work better

Makers

Do something else and want to get stuck in? Join the community!

Events

The best way to hear about upcoming events is to join our Meetup group. You should also Join the Community to hear about what we're up to.

Online Community

Our WhatsApp group is the best way to get stuck in for now.

Who's behind it?

ToryTechs was founded in 2019 by Joe Reeve, a Fellow of The London College of Political Technologists, founder of HackThePress.Org, and general open-source contributor.
